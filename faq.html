FAQ

I'm selling my house, do I need an EPC?

Yes you do! Your Agent may offer you this service. Check on how much it will cost, even if they say 'Oh, we only charge you what we get charged'. That's fine but sometimes the Agent get's charged too much. It can pay to shop around.

I'm a Landlord, do I need an EPC?

Yes you do! If you use an Agent, they may offer you this service. Check on how much it will cost, even if they say 'Oh, we only charge you what we get charged' That's fine but sometimes the Agent get's charged too much. It can pay to shop around.

I own my house and not thinking of selling or renting, do I need an EPC?

No you don't BUT having an EPC carried out on your property can point out areas where efficiency can be improved and thus save you money 

I have an old EPC but I have had improvemets done to my house, can my EPC be updated?

A new EPC test would have to be done. This is a 'must' if you're selling or renting even if your old EPC is still in date. House Hunters will look at ???? and will not be aware of the improvements.
